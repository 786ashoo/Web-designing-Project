Media Affairs

CONTACT US : For Recruitment / Induction in PAF, please visit www.joinpaf.gov.pk
The Directorate of Media Affairs is established at Air Headquarters Islamabad to act as a linkage between Pakistan Air Force and the media, both during peace and war time. The media persons can approach the Directorate on round the clock basis to get correct and authenticated information about any event or happening in Pakistan Air Force. The Directorate also issues official press statements about such events.


The media persons interested in any research work pertaining to PAF can also seek assistance and guidance from the Directorate.
